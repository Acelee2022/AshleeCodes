Interactive Pomodoro Timer:

An interactive timer with a simple user interface featuring Start, Stop, and Reset buttons for real-time updates. The timer’s gradient background animation pauses when the timer is stopped, adding visual effects to the user experience. Built-in alerts notify users upon work session completion.

Supply Chain Analytics Project – Makeup Products

I performed an in-depth exploratory data analysis (EDA) on a dataset containing over 20 supply chain features, such as product performance, shipping costs, lead time, and defect rates. Through the analysis, I was able to determine which suppliers are the most efficient, which delivery methods and costs are most appropriate for the company, and which cosmetics provide the highest revenue. While developing this dashboard, I used a variety of visualization styles, labels, filters, and details.
